Claudio Micarelli (Jasonic Voy) joined Dr Voy september 2013 at the bass.

Régis Pelletti (Reg Voy) joined Dr Voy september 2013 at the drum.

After "Time Runs Away" and "Kill The Angel" Dr Voy begin April 2012 with "That's All Fake", the 3rd album composed of 10 tracks completely rock'n' roll.
After 3 years, the public will discover carefully refined compositions, beaten rhythms, authentic riffs.
The album is a self-production just like the previous one, distributed by COD&S in the Benelux and in France but also available on line via Hysterias.
